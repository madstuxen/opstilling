Recover

This is your data your words. Download it anytime and keep a personal copy. If you ever switch platforms or want to move your journal to a new account, simply upload your file and pick up where you left off. You can download once per hour. Uploaded data is merged into your active account — entries may be overwritten where dates overlap.
