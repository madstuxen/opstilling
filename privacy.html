Privacy Policy — Me & Them

Last updated: February 2026

Me & Them is developed by Grafiskkooperativ. We respect your privacy and only collect data necessary to operate the app.

What we collect

We collect your email and login information when you create an account via Apple or Google. We store your journal entries and exercise responses in our database. We store your subscription status.

What we don’t do

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your personal entries with others.

Third parties

We use Firebase (Google) for login and data storage, and RevenueCat and Apple for subscription management.

Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at: info@grafiskkooperativ.dk
