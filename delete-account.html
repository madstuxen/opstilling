Delete account

Once your subscription has been cancelled, your account will automatically be deleted 6 months later.

You can delete your account directly from the app.

If you don’t delete it, you can sign in before the end of the 6 months and continue your journal.

Cancel your subscription first and delete the account afterwards, or it will automatically be deleted.
