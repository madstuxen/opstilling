About the app

Me & Them is a self-development journal that trains your self-awareness and empathy. Through small exercises, you learn to see yourself from the outside and others from the inside.

Having trouble with the app?

Please describe your issue and include which device you are using and whether it’s an Apple or Android device, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

If you have any suggestions or features you would like to see or improve, you are most welcome to write to me.
